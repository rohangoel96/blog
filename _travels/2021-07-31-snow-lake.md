description: The Alpine Lakes Wilderness showcases some of the most astonishing terrain in the entire Cascade Range. More than 700 lakes and mountain ponds dot the glacier-carved terrain of this wilderness. Since it is too risky to it in snow, due to avalanche risk, we decided to hike the most famous Snow Lake!

Snow lake has translucent depths and is tucked underneath the uppermost 2,000 feet of the Chair Peak. Aptly called snowlake because the snow here melts into the lake until July!!!!
|
Starting the hike with a gradual climb into the thick forests ...
|
 ... and then some lush green temperate rain-forests?
|
Not long before the trail gets exponentially more rugged and steep - however, you can also see Chair Peak standing tall and rising over the far side of the valley.
|
Suuuuppper stony along with being a pain in the ass and legs - imagining this area in snow gives me chills!
|
Okay, that''s some high quality kitchen grade granite behind me.

First glimpse of the lake down below and you can also see into the Alpine Lakes Wilderness!

Convocation @ BPGC

Goa, India - August 05, 2018
The convocation day brings with itself a roller coaster of emotions and is a time for celebration and joy. The time when the grads don the graduation gown and wear the mortarboards as a sign of (academic) excellence. It is without a doubt, one of the most important moments in a person's life.

Presenting you: Engineer Rohan Goel, having passed the course B.E. (Hons) in Computer Science with Distinction. Lulz

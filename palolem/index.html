Pristine Palolem

Goa, India - March 01, 2015
Palolem Beach is one of the most unspoiled beach I have ever been to India. Being very far down south, not many people flock here which instantly makes it my favorite beach.

A beach where you can click your Goan Postcard pics probably.

The butterfly "island" just off the beach which is accessible on foot during times of low tide.

Doesn't it look like straight out of a bollywood movie set?

With such calmness all around, it seems to be a perfectly place to meditate :P

If you keep walking south across the beach, during times of low tide you can access the rocky end to the land.

Kayaking @ Palolem

Observe how the picturesque palm trees run parallel to the coast augmenting the beauty of the beach

The beautiful view of the horizon

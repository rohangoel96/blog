Netravali Fall Trek

Netravali, Goa, India - October 02, 2014
2014 is the first year of college and whats a better way to bond than going on an excursion together with the whole lot from the hostel? We decided to trek to the Savari Waterfall at Netrvali Wildlife Sanctuary.

The state of Goa which is famous for it's beaches and party life also possesses some hidden treasures like this.

So here we start the trek. @TejasAgrawal @AmolSaket @ChintanBetrabet @VarunGoyal

One needs to cross many streams during the trek. The wet stones are super slippery so forming a human chains increases the chances of survival :P Missing primary school?
@PulkitAgarwal @DebadityaBasu @ShikharSaluja @KunwarjeetChawla @ShreyasSukrit @ShubhVaccher

After an hour and half of a trek, we are greeted by the charm of the waterfall.

The fall forms a natural pool at it's base and drenching in water to get some relief from the scorthing heat of Goa is blissful.

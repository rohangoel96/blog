Arambol Beach

Goa, India
October 10, 2016
Arambol is the northenmost beach of Goa which is pretty pristine and naturally attracts a lot of foreignours.

After walking more than 2km along the coast, we found the peace of this sort of secret beach away from our hectic lives.

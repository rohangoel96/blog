December 20, 2019

Themed after the history and culture of California, Disney''s California Adventure Park celebrates ''adventure of the state'' through various Disney characters. Guess you just come here and you have seen the best of California at one place?
The grand entrance to the 70+ acre property.

Cars Land is designed as a life-size model the town of Radiator Springs from the Cars films
Radiator Springs Racers is one of the most famous attractions of the park with scenic drive through the mountains.

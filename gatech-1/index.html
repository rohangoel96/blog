Grinding Grad School

Atlanta, USA
October 10, 2019
Everyone knows and expects that graduate school is tough, but trust me that it's harder and stressful than you think it is. Very different from my experience in Europe, colleges in the USA, I guess are probably worth all the hype.

Over a century old, Tech Tower is a landmark of tradition and school spirit at Georgia Tech. It is the iconic representation of the university and of higher education in Atlanta.

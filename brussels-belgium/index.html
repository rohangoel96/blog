Brussels - The European capital

Brussels, Belgium - October 13, 2017
Brussels, the de facto capital of the European Union was one of the most multilingual cities I have ever witnessed. Most of people spoke all of English, French and Dutch fluently.

One can probably see the whole of europe - atleast the touristy spots in all of europe in this de facto EU capital. _MiniEurope

Atomium which represents a unit cell of the iron cystal (magnified 165 billion times) was actually made of Al_minium. Moreover, it was designed to showcase the advent of atomic age - iron cannot be used in nuclear reactions.

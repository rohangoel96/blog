Universal at Hollywood

Hollywood, LA, USA
December 24, 2019
View as slideshow
The original Universal Studios at Hollywood is aptly called ''The Entertainment Capital of LA'' is one of the oldest and most famous Hollywood film studios still in use. It is also the world's largest motion picture studio!
The studio was initially created to offer tours of the real Universal Studios sets and later turned into a full fledged theme park before going global!

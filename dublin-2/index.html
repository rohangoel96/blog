Buh bye Dublin

Dublin, Ireland
July 23, 2017
So here comes to end my first "international trip". I couldn't travel outside of Ireland (even to the northern part :() thanks to the visa restrictions, but I am proud to have travelled a lot in Ireland and made so many new friends. I'll surely miss the super hospitable and ever smiling nature of the Irish. It was a great experience, both at work and culturally. Wouldn't think twice before coming to Dublin again.

At TrinityCollge - the most prestigious university in Ireland and the world. There have been so many film shootings at this place.

The very famous library at Trinity, which also houses the BookOfKells.

Trust me that these college premises were established more than 4 centuries ago and are still shining like new.

When your colleagues (read professors) are cool enough to invite you to their monthly trekking trip @ WicklowMountains. With @ThibautLust @AndreRios @VirginieToulemonde

When you couldn't feel your legs but can't complain because PROFESSORS!!! But this top view was probably worth it

The home to the best Irish whiskey in the World - Jameson. Ireland surely has different sort of mueseums - mostly on alcohol :P

Tasting and learning to differentiate between 3 types of whiskeys : Scottish, Irish and American.

Pride (LGBT) parade in Dublin - amongst other firsts for me

Too many colors in one place?

The Dublin Zoo was by far the best and the largest zoo I have seen my entire life

African savanah in the super cold Ireland? @ DublinZoo

The "red" temple bar with its cobbled streets is the most famous to get drunk in Ireland

Finally some real (non alcohol related) museum in Ireland. Whoosh!

Here comes another one, the wax museum.

DIT FOCAS - my workplace :)

This is the place I used to sit and work. I liked the very open and non-cubicle culture here.

Yes, I was a (21 year old) staff. Yo!

Fortunately or unfortunately, my lab was located right in the (expensive) city center.

Cheers Dublin! Thank you so much for the wonderful memories. Will miss you :(

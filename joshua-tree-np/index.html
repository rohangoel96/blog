Weird flora like twisted and bristled Joshua Trees along with barbed cholla cacti somehow blooming in the desert. The name of the national park is derived from distinctive Joshua Tree, a tall-growing variety of the yucca genus that grows prevalently within its boundaries.
It is a type of yucca, a desert plant with unique, branched, and often spiky leaves, known for its resilience and adaptation to arid environments.
